ABOUT THE COMPANY

CreditLink is a marketplace lending platform aiming to provide easy and affordable access to capital for businesses. Many companies struggle to obtain cost-effective capital, often facing high bond issuance costs or demanding capital providers.Our goal is to offer quick, inexpensive capital access while ensuring investor principal safety. With 9 out of 10 businesses failing due to capital challenges, it's evident that the global financial system hinders growth, particularly for SMEs. We believe in empowering individuals with diverse skills and ideas, often overlooked in traditional investment circles. By enabling easier access to capital, we can foster economic growth through SME expansion.
